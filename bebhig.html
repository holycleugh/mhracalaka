Eva nagrzinogh.

Na grúgyúdh den̄jh bhrenzer, “Bvigdogh yádjbhogh, bvigdogh jmurdedogh.

Mamb; dédh, dam; boṅ, ghó, goṅ, bhgurn̄; jen̄.
    Gazṅam etser edbh yr gazṅam gnotser gnot,
        Gva gazṅam jhájhijser dvama yr gazṅam dvamadher edbh.

Ye jengi Cuinzi, “véjhi dag ghudh, véjhi gray barm.

Gheynabhin, “drukkiṅ bhnídogh, gandig ginogh.

Dhed grodijhi, “ṅedo n̄odhogh gbúra, ṅedo bnid jhujhra.

Din̄ ghegogghi, “boga dejé nebharjgi ṅoṅrṅad.

Din̄ dhemvogghi, “boga rgujhgiṅṅad dedgiṅṅat, jidhuyogh gurnogh eva jmurjhi.”

Tharogh bhren Nagárjhunjhi, Ghariddhoterjhi, Ghibnzinajhi, Borbhíryorjhi;
    Godbhridjhi, Godrobjhi, Gurdjhi, Gharbhredjhi.
