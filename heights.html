Two sons.

It is finished. For it is written, “And seeing the multitudes, he went up into a mountain”.

Woe to you, for it is written, “Lord, even the devils are subject unto us through thy name”.

Put up again thy sword into his place.

Eli, Eli, lama sabachthani? that is to say, My God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me?

Thou hast heard that it was said, “Ye are the light of the world”;

For it is written, “And he shall send his angels with a great sound of a trumpet, and they shall gather together his elect from the four winds, from one end of heaven to the other”;

And it is written, “And whosoever shall exalt himself shall be humbled; and he that shall humble himself shall be exalted”.

But behold, it is written, “‘the sun will be darkened, and the moon will not give its light’”.

And behold, it is written, “Thy brother is come; and thy father has killed the fatted calf, because he hath received him safe and sound”;

And it is written, “for this thy brother was dead, and is alive again; and was lost, and is found”.

Raca.
