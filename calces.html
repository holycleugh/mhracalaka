How the turntables of Achilles have.


Godlike Achilles, his heart gripped with terror as glorious Hector was,
Riding through hill and dale ’gainst his ascending fate reckoned by Peleus son,
Godlike Achilles, did cower now, ugly in visage and broken in
Stature. So we, with bold Priam, do kneel before thrice-equal Hecate,
Dining with Hector, with sky in sea, watching the starkest of contrasts from
Daybreaks opaquest white ocean clear to midnights moonlit-black billow waves
Mark it our equal in likenesses.
