Five thrisand years ago,

The gap belooked, growing again
From nought, becomes all, now inholds
Everything bright, and Blazer, Beamer,
This stone selfsteering, and her starheart.

She twice thirsts thricegreating,
Ran and Waylind, and Easter unrought,
Man and Ym the earthwain fare
To fangle knotbrains bending back.

Look! a hungry hoard of knots bends,
Becoming green, and cast sourness
On land and in the seething seas,
Coldly creeping slowly, saying,

“Signy seething samewise sunders,
‘Ym after now undoes every thing
Ment of Man, and inwise they andgo,’
And gaingives glowing Geofons gift.”

Now a dotsome lifeling brainbends,
Chewing crunching, eats a redling
Soaked in its umsour, and swallows and
Breathes, and the lift seeps dry anew.

Hungry worts and hungry wights
Heap onwith now on Waylinds house,
Withweighted wights, they heave, and walk
Meanwhile worts root, and then they rise.

Look! the overgreat wort, unbearsome
In her work, allintakes the welkin and
Frosts the far fathoms of greenlings,
Unbegun boast bursting, bellowing,

“Signy seething samewise sunders,
‘Ym after now undoes every thing

The spooky bones of malm and dust.

The seven motes of malm, they are ironmalm, oremalm, ercmalm, tinmalm, goldmalm, quickmalm, leadmalm. And the seven motes of dust, they are glazedust, coaldust, flintdust, waxdust, brimdust, atterdust, stinkdust. So, there are three kinds of stuff, and they are the the malm, the root, the dust. Each kind of stuff works a wending of its thing. The root wends only, whilst the malm and the dust wend otherly. And lastly, each mote of malm matches a onesome mote of dust. So, here are the matches.

  • Quickmalm matches brimdust (suchness of one).
  • Oremalm matches stinkdust.
  • Ironmalm matches coaldust.
  • Tinmalm matches glazedust.
  • Leadmalm matches flintdust.
  • Ercmalm matches atterdust.
  • Goldmalm matches waxdust.
