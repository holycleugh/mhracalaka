All kinds of bodies are gathered.


The standard enumeration of the central catalogue is given now.

The ox seal.For strength and steadfastness.
The womb seal.For shelter and cover.
The bough seal.For striking and hitting.
The boundary seal.For transformations of character.
The welkin seal.For meekness.
The hook seal.For rapture.
The blade seal.For sorrow and agony.
The barrier seal.For cleavings of all kinds.
The ring seal.For to-morrows coming sun.
The hand seal.For the universe.
The palm seal.For the self.
The flail seal.For the harvest.
The water seal.For renewal of spoiled fruit.
The snake seal.For perfect wisdom.
The fish seal.For Lotans coiled serpent.
The eye seal.For insight.
The tongue seal.For admittance into the conscious world.
The seal of seals.For selflessness.
The piercing seal.For awareness and quietness.
The crown seal.For arrogance.
The bone seal.For all kinds of destruction.
The mark seal.For clusters and gathered things.
