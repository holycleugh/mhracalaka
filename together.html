English.

In the grove of scarlet thunderdrills.

The ring of liftly peers who journeyed to learn
The landed craft were soon deluded by hubris.
Envy and resentment storied of war,
Till violence joined two crowds of exiles in toil.

Anglish.

In the grove of the red woodpeckers.

Of the lifting loop of louds cleft, one came to learn
The lowerlandly laws, and then came to crave things, and clinged to them.
The bothwise bond between broke, and they became bitter,
And hatred halved them again, and soon sixteen soughs said things.

True shape of the hidden song.
