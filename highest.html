The true suchness of the unflawing tung.

This is the suchness of the unflawing tung, that is free from every flaw.
Its loudhoard is the rimefield $\F_{2^4} = \{a + bt + ct^2 + dt^3 \mid a, b, c, d \in \F_2\}$,
And its wordhoard is the rimering $\F_{2^4}[x] = \{\alpha_0 + \alpha_1x + \dots + \alpha_nx^n \mid \alpha_i \in \F_{2^4}, n \in \N\}$.
Look, every loud of every word grows out of the untwo splitting field.
The greater $\F_2[t]/\langle{t^4+t+1\rangle}[x]$ from the lesser harvest,
And the lesser $\F_2[t]/\langle{t^4+t^3+1\rangle}[x]$ from the greater harvest.

Everything is two and everything is one, and everything is two in one.
Everything is a name or a deed, and every name becomes a deed,
And every deed becomes a name, and every name and deed shapes and shows it.

The name has bodily shape on earth, so it reckons its roots.
But the deed has no body, and instead shifts its roots,
By the tidethread $x \mapsto x^i$ or $x^a \mapsto x^c$, or the tidestead $x^a \mapsto x^{a+i}$ or $x^a \mapsto x^c$.

The first word to be uttered was $t^2 + (t^2 + t^3)x + (t^2 + t^3 + t^5)x^2 + (t^2 + t^3 + t^5 + t^7)x^3 + \dots$,
And the last word to be uttered will be $1 + (1+t)x + (1+t+t^2)x^2 + (1+t+t^2+t^3)x^3 + \dots$,
One handful of motes glows with every hue and reaches everywhere the eye sees.

The bodily name shifts to the shapeword, $\sum a_ix^i \mapsto \sum a_i + x^2\sum a_ix^i$,
The shape shifts to the wayword, $\sum a_ix^i \mapsto \prod a_i + x^2\sum a_ix^i$,
The way shifts to the deedword, $\sum a_ix^i \mapsto \coprod a_i + x^2\sum a_ix^i$, where $\coprod a_i = e^{\prod\ln{a_i}}$.

See now that the bodiless deed grows foreverwhere.

The righteous body of the unflawing tung.
