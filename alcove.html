Charitopsychai.

The grove of scarlet thunderdrills.

ENTER Hudhud, Tawus.

ENTER Andalib.

Hudhud: Hail, o cloud of fire! and let us go
To thee, that we may know the motions of
Thy tungly dance which carries to and fro
Those rhymes without sea, earth, and lift above.

Andalib: These waves unshaping splash and spill adrift,
These rays unruining render ev’ry shade;
These whorls ungrounding swirl around and shift;
Arrive, and listen to the serenade.

The tung uncome rests not in chasm or cave,
Nor flies beneath the seas plumage of noise,
Nor scales the summit of the loft concave
Except she spreads her wings in mirrored poise.

Hudhud: Now— speak, Tawus, divine! this tung is thine,
Thine to define! ’tis etched in nak’d design.

EXIT Hudhud.

Tawus: Look, I feel the glyphs carved in my skull,
These thousand tones, I touch up to their pull—

Andalibs alcove. Unbright, she curves without our expanse, lies
Outwise our sunstruck sphere in silence, unneeding of torchwick.
Stealthèd by eight thousand eight thousand bees and a score score score butterflies,
She, with her swarms of daggers unscatt’rable, returns us aphasic.
Here, twice-unwelcome Ghurab and plume-fingered Buma acrobates
Sunder a chasm thiswise and nextwise through smothering densities:
Twenty-one score lepidopterans and nine hundred apians rise,
Numbering few but thund’ring with fury, they shower manipoeic
Nectar undoing against the wanderer, her selfness to disguise,
Betwixt these twin meshes affixèd who answers the call of the liminic.

Andalib: So she is Saqr; from springs flight, pure and kind,
To yules auspicious swoop, she doth pursue
A sacred twain of nests, their keepers twined
In sacred conference pairwise four per two:

“The ring of liftly peers who journeyed to learn
The landed craft were soon deluded by hubris.
Envy and resentment storied of war,
Till violence joined two crowds of exiles in toil.”

The final cut rends—

Tawus: And a light! it ends—

Andalib: And neither doth the traveller lose her way.

Tawus: Eight beacons gleam, each perfect beam unbends—

Andalib: Eight docks beneath, each ship in sheath away—

They EXIT.

ENTER Tahwt.

And all of this did happen. And mirad, soon after, Tahwt began to walk this path.
