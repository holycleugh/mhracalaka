Charitopsychai.

The grove of scarlet thunderdrills.

ENTER Hudhud, Tawus.

ENTER Andalib.

Hudhud: Hail, o cloud of fire! and let us go
To thee, that we may know the motions of
Thy tungly dance which carries to and fro
Those rhymes without sea, earth, and lift above.

Andalib: These waves unshaping splash and spill adrift,
These rays unruining render ev’ry shade;
These whorls ungrounding swirl around and shift;
Arrive, and listen to the serenade.

The tung uncome rests not in chasm or cave,
Nor flies beneath the seas plumage of noise,
Nor scales the summit of the loft concave
Except she spreads her wings in mirrored poise.

Hudhud: Now— speak, Tawus, divine! this tung is thine,
Thine to define! ’tis etched in nak’d design.

EXIT Hudhud.

Tawus: Look, I feel the glyphs carved in my skull;
My claws do touch before their pull—

Andalibs alcove dimly rests without
The hav’n of altered tung, where lurk outwise
Eight thousand times eight thousand bees about
And twenty twenty twenty butterflies.

Nine hundred apians beyond this glome
Confound and cloud, and swarm, and stab the mind;
Twenty-one score more lepidopterans roam
In subtle light, and contort every kind.

Betwixt this mesh affixèd arcs unvexed
The image of Ghurab twice-watching, bound
With mortal skin; and Buma twice-unsexed,
In sideways stance half crowned and half uncrowned.

Andalib: Saqr is she called; from eager springtime flight
To regal yuletide swoop, she doth pursue
A sacred twain of nests, alike not quite,
And numb’ring two times two times two times two.

See, with each nest an everlasting plane
Of eightfold gaze and never-passing quake;
And half, Qadus and Qadus bend their wain,
While half remain unstruck in their outtake;

And half advance against that twain marquee,
While half withdraw to quieter plumage;
And half see ’neath their seat the rippling sea,
While half are plunged under its dark visage.

The final cut rends—

Tawus: And a light! it ends—

Andalib: And neither doth the traveller lose her way—

Tawus: Eight beacons gleam—
Each perfect beam—
Unbends—

Andalib: Eight docks beneath—
Each ship in sheath—
Away—

They EXIT.

ENTER Tahwt.

And all of this did happen. And mirad, soon after, Tahwt began to walk this path.
